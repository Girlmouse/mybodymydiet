Disclaimers & Data Transparency

Not medical advice. My Body My Diet is a personal diet companion tool. Nothing in this app is intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making significant changes to your diet.

Nutrition data. Nutritional values are sourced from the USDA FoodData Central database (fdc.nal.usda.gov), the official US Department of Agriculture nutrition reference. Values are estimates and may vary based on preparation method, brand, portion size, and product variation.

Your data. All personal data you enter — your food rules, pantry, meals, and logs — is stored securely in your account. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising. You can export or delete everything at any time from your Profile.

Food sensitivities. The ingredient checker flags items based on what you tell us to avoid. It is not a substitute for careful label reading, especially for serious allergies. Always verify ingredients yourself.

Contact. Questions or concerns? Reach us at contact@lunabellegames.com
