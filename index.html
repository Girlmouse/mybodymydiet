Start Here

Feeling overwhelmed by all the noise around food? Here's the simplest path in. No pressure, no timeline.

1
Build your list
Write down what feels good in your body, what sometimes works, and what never does. Not based on rules — based on experience.
2
Stock your pantry
Once you know what works, keep a variety of those things around. Not perfectly — just consistently enough that you have options.
3
Put meals together
Simple combinations from what you already know works. No new recipes required at first — just eating what your body recognizes.
4
Let it become instinct
After a while you won't need the list or the booklet. You'll just know. That's the whole point.
