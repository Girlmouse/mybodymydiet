About Me

The story behind this site

Hi, I'm Joana.

A middle-aged woman doing her best to survive this reality.

A lot of things have changed throughout my life, but one thing has always stayed constant: my desire to live true to myself and take care of the people I love. I'm happy to report I've been lucky so far to do just that, and hope to continue doing it for as long as this universe lets me. Finding new and easy ways to take care of myself has been my mission.

I also love writing, video games, cooking, comedy, and helping other — and that's how this site was born. Out of a passion to share what I've learned, and the hope that it helps someone else even a little bit.

Thanks for stopping by.
