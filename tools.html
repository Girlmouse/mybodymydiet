Things I made for myself first, then cleaned up to share. No accounts, no tracking, no apps to download. Your data stays on your device — save it, print it, email it to yourself. That's it.

Diet · Worksheet
My Food List Worksheet
Your personal food rules, based on facts from your food sensitivity test — not guesswork. Sort foods into OK / Sometimes / No based on how they actually make you feel. Save it, print it, make it yours.
Body · Journal
My Lady Journal
A printable period and symptom journal — one calendar page for marking your days and one notes page for everything else, for every month. Pick your start month and year, print it or save it as a PDF, and keep it somewhere only you can find it.
